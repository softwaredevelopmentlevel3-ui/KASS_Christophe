KASS Admissions

Welcome

Welcome to Karenge Adventist Secondary School (KASS). We provide quality education, strong discipline, and moral values to prepare students for a successful future.

Admission Requirements

  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report / Results
  • 2 Passport-size Photos
  • Transfer Letter (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Pay application fee
  4. Attend interview or entrance exam
  5. Receive admission confirmation

Important Dates (2026 Intake)

  • Applications Open: 6 January 2026
  • Applications Close: 15 March 2026
  • Entrance Exams: 22 March 2026
  • Term Begins: 20 April 2026

Fees Structure (2026)

  • Registration Fee: 20,000 RWF
  • Tuition Fee (per term): 150,000 RWF
  • Boarding Fee (per term): 100,000 RWF
  • Uniform: 30,000 RWF
